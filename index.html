Who We Are

Bhubaneswar's Most Trusted Auto Care Center

Founded on September 10, 2021, by Satyaprakash Sahoo, The Needs was born from a simple vision: to give every car owner access to honest, expert, and comprehensive vehicle care — all in one place.

Located on the Cuttack to Puri Bypass Road in Jharpada, Bhubaneswar, we have grown to become one of the most sought-after auto service centers in the region, earning a 4.5-star rating from 91+ satisfied customers. We provide all types of 4-wheeler services under one roof.

Meet the Founder

Satyaprakash
Sahoo

Founder & Chief Automotive Technician

Satyaprakash Sahoo is the driving force behind The Needs. With a lifelong passion for automobiles and a deep commitment to customer satisfaction, he established The Needs in 2021 with a clear mission: to provide every car owner in Bhubaneswar with world-class automotive care at honest prices.

His hands-on approach and technical expertise set the standard for every service performed at the workshop. Satyaprakash personally oversees quality control and ensures that each vehicle leaves the garage in its best possible condition.

Under his leadership, The Needs has grown from a small garage to a full-service automotive center trusted by thousands of car owners across Odisha.
